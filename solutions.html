Solution overview

Two routes, two mechanisms, one qualification framework.

SolarEX v2.0 is intentionally positioned as a mechanism-led platform. The user should not ask which coating is universally best, but which mechanism is better suited to the site’s dominant fouling profile and economic logic.

SolarEX Quartz | SiO₂

Hydrophobic and oleophobic easy-clean layer

  • Low-adhesion surface for dust, sand, and mineral spotting
  • Updated source deck states 100–150 nm layer thickness
  • Manual application 15–17 ml/m² and industrial 18–20 ml/m² in deck framing
  • Europe ROI framing: 147 days

SolarEX Titan | TiO₂

Photocatalytic and superhydrophilic route

  • Breaks down organic contamination under UV exposure
  • Promotes sheet-flow rinsing after activation
  • PV³ study reports +5.15% average gain over 360 days
  • High-insolation ROI framing: 87 days
